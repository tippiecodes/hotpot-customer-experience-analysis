# Hotpot Customer Experience Analysis

This project analyses customer experience, loyalty, and retention in the hotpot restaurant industry using survey data and customer journey mapping.

It explores how customer perceptions evolve across the pre-dining, dining, and post-dining stages, and how specific touchpoints influence satisfaction, return intent, and advocacy.

---

## Project Scope
- Customer satisfaction and loyalty drivers
- Service quality, wait time, pricing, and dining experience factors
- End-to-end customer journey across key touchpoints
- Opportunities to improve retention and overall experience

---

## Approach
- Customer experience survey analysis
- Exploratory analysis to identify behavioural patterns and drivers
- Journey mapping to capture emotions, pain points, and expectations
- Insight synthesis into actionable business recommendations

---

- Data storytelling and insight communication
